Laboratory Department

The objective of the department is to provide complete, accurate and efficient laboratory services. It is the goal of the department to obtain accurate and reliable laboratory results using state of the art machines and competent staff, and to deliver these results to the patients or clients on time. It aims to make certain that professional practice is implemented at all times, and that reliable and timely services are provided to achieve patient satisfaction. It endeavors to continuously meet or much more, to surpass the expectation of the patients, clients and the members of the health care team that acquire the particular services provided for them by the department. The department of Laboratory Services provides stat and routine clinical laboratory and anatomic pathology services for hospital in-patients and out-patients, clinic and other non-OLMCMC healthcare providers. Operating Room Complex

The operating room (O.R.) is a facility within a hospital specializing in different surgical procedures. Patients are being treated inside O.R. through surgical operations under anesthesia. Asides from the surgical procedures OR provides special care which includes IV fluid administration, medications, electro-cautery machine. Under O.R., the hospital has a Recovery Room (R.R.), known as Post Anesthesia Care Unit (PACU). It houses post – operative patients for a minimum of two hours or until the time the anesthesia had wear off. Moreover, there is a Central Supply Room (CSR) inside the theatre, where all the medicines and supplies are readily available once needed.
